What We Learned

Working as a Team

Even though we are from different countries and backgrounds, we found ways to work together effectively. Each person brought unique skills: Muhammad's experience with training, Syeda's problem-solving approach, Lilian's data skills, and Stefan's coding abilities.

Why Subject Analysis?

We picked subject counting because it gives clear, easy-to-understand results. Plus it is practical. Libraries and bookstores could actually use this kind of analysis to understand what topics are popular.

Everyone learned something outside their main role. The data people learned about algorithms, the algorithm people learned about data cleaning and we all learned about web development and teamwork.
